## The one-line proposal
In about 9 months, deliver a small self-service flow — **request → approval → booking → central invoice** — for a pilot group of NGO/humanitarian organisations. It reuses Key Travel's existing booking system, adds easy-to-configure organisations, travel rules and approvals, and connects to Microsoft Business Central for finance.
